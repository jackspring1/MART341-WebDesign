The Artist

Home a.k.a Randy Goffe

A Quick Bio

Randy Goffe aka HOME is an american musician from Punta Gorda in Florida (Miami). His electronic music is inspired by the late 70's and the 80's. The artist is associated to musical styles like Vaporwave, Chiptunes and necessarily Synthwave which he's one of the new pioneers.
